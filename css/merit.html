Project Overview

The Agritrace project aims to develop a transparent agricultural product tracking platform that enables stakeholders to track the journey of agricultural products from farm to table. By leveraging blockchain technology, the platform will ensure data integrity, traceability, and efficiency in the supply chain, ultimately enhancing food safety, reducing waste, and increasing trust among stakeholders. The project aligns with our organization's goal of leveraging emerging technologies for social impact and tapping into the growing demand for sustainable and transparent supply chains.

Join Us

Project Objectives

  • Develop a user-friendly platform for tracking agricultural products throughout the supply chain.
  • Implement blockchain technology to ensure data integrity and traceability of product information.
  • Enhance food safety by enabling stakeholders to identify and address potential risks in the supply chain.
  • Reduce food waste by providing real-time visibility into product location and condition.
  • Increase trust among stakeholders by promoting transparency and accountability in the agricultural industry.

Project Scope

The project scope includes:

  • Researching and identifying relevant data sources for agricultural product tracking.
  • Designing and developing a user interface that is intuitive and accessible to stakeholders.
  • Implementing backend infrastructure to support data storage, retrieval, and blockchain integration.
  • Conducting testing and quality assurance to ensure the platform meets defined standards.
  • Deploying the platform to a test environment for user feedback and optimization.
  • Providing training and support to stakeholders for platform adoption and usage.

Key Deliverables

  • Requirements documentation outlining project goals, scope, and timeline.
  • User interface designs and prototypes for the Agritrace platform.
  • Developed backend infrastructure supporting data storage and blockchain integration.
  • Tested and optimized platform deployed to a test environment for user feedback.
  • Training materials and user manuals for stakeholders.
  • Project review report documenting lessons learned and best practices.
