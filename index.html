Our products:

Cassava
Cassava, scientifically known as Manihot esculenta, is a woody shrub native to South America. It is widely cultivated as an annual crop in tropical and subtropical regions for its edible starchy tuberous root, a major source of carbohydrates.
Rice
Rice, scientifically known as Oryza sativa, is one of the world's most important staple foods, particularly in Asia. It provides sustenance to more than half of the global population.
Yam
Yams, scientifically known as Dioscorea species, are starchy tuber vegetables native to Africa and Asia. They are distinct from sweet potatoes and are a significant source of carbohydrates and essential nutrients in many parts of the world.
Potatoes
Potatoes, scientifically known as Solanum tuberosum, are tuberous crops native to the Andes region of South America. They are one of the most widely grown and consumed crops globally, serving as a vital food source and an important agricultural commodity.

What we do

-Source (Farm) name,location and consumer rating
-Some documentation on the farm practices and inputs that have been used
-Names, location and consumer ratings of processing firms
- Materials, methods and duration used for processing, storage and packaging
- Mode and duration of transport
- Farm gate price

The objectives of this project: Develop a user-friendly platform for tracking agricultural products throughout the supply chain. Implement blockchain technology to ensure data integrity and traceability of product information. Enhance food safety by enabling stakeholders to identify and address potential risks in the supply chain.Increase trust among stakeholders by promoting transparency and accountability in the agricultural industry.

Are you passionate about transforming the agricultural supply chain? Join Agritrace and be part of a revolution that ensures transparency, safety, and sustainability from farm to table.

Overview!

The Agritrace project aims to develop a transparent agricultural product tracking platform that enables stakeholders to track the journey of agricultural products from farm to table. By leveraging blockchain technology, the platform will ensure data integrity, traceability, and efficiency in the supply chain, ultimately enhancing food safety, reducing waste, and increasing trust among stake holders
